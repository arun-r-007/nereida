Threats Facing Our Oceans

Understanding these challenges is the first step toward solving them

Climate Change

Rising temperatures affect marine life migration and health, causing coral bleaching and altering ecosystems.

Pollution

Plastic waste and chemical runoff harm marine ecosystems, entering the food chain and affecting all ocean life.

Overfishing

Unsustainable practices reduce fish populations, disrupt food chains, and damage marine habitats.

Habitat Loss

Coral reefs and mangroves are being degraded at an alarming rate, destroying critical marine ecosystems.
